This Privacy Policy describes how TileDrift – Sliding Puzzle ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain data:

AdMob (Google LLC) — We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads:

Google's data collection is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. You can review it at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. User-Provided Content

If you choose to use a photo from your device's gallery as a puzzle image, that photo is processed entirely on-device and is never uploaded, stored externally, or shared with any third party. We do not have access to your photos.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or any account information

  • Any biometric or sensitive personal data

4. Local Storage

The App stores your game preferences and personal best scores locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated Everyone (3+) and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertising displayed through AdMob complies with applicable children's privacy regulations.

6. Advertising

The App is free to use and is supported by advertising through Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized based on your Advertising ID unless you have opted out of ad personalization in your device settings.

To opt out of personalized ads on iOS:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
